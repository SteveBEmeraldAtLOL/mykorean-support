Account & Data Deletion – MyKorean App

To request that your account and all associated data be deleted, please email us at:

koreanapp.help@gmail.com

Subject: Delete My Account

What will be deleted:

  • Your Firebase authentication account (email and login credentials)
  • Any progress or settings stored in the app under your user ID

What will NOT be deleted:

  • Anonymous analytics data not linked to your identity
  • System logs or backups (automatically purged after 30 days)

Timeframe:

Your data will be deleted within 7 business days of receiving your request.
